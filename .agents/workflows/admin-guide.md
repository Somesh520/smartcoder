Welcome to the heart of the platform. Follow these steps to manage users, verify payments, and monitor system health.

## 1. Accessing the Command Center
Navigate to the **Profile Section** and click on the **Admin Dashboard** button (only visible to authorized admins).

## 2. Managing Users
Use the `All Users` tab to perform granular actions:
- **Set Credits**: Click `Edit Credits` to manually adjust a user's wallet.
- **Toggle Premium**: Click the `FREE/PRO` badge to instantly change account tier.
- **Account Moderation**: Use the `X` button to delete accounts if necessary.

## 3. Verifying Payments
Check the `Payment Requests` tab daily:
1. Compare the **Transaction ID** with your banking/gateway receipts.
2. Click **Verify & Approve** to instantly credit the user.
3. Use **Reject** if the transaction is fraudulent or mismatched.

## 4. Monitoring Activity
The `Activity` tab provides a live feed of:
- Problems currently being solved.
- Match results and winners.
- Room participation density.

## 5. System Health
Check the `Overview` tab for real-time aggregation:
- **Total Revenue**: Sum of all approved payments.
- **Online Users**: Live socket connection count.
- **Active Rooms**: Current competitive sessions.
